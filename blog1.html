Dive into Innovation: The AI in Action Google Cloud Hackathon

Learn how to register, access key resources, and build innovative projects with Google Cloud, MongoDB, and GitLab.

Ready to build the future of AI on the cloud? We're thrilled to announce the AI in Action Hackathon, a unique opportunity to unleash your creativity and build with three cutting-edge AI platforms.

This hackathon brings together the power of Google Cloud, MongoDB, and GitLab, offering distinct challenges for you to dive deep into these exciting technologies. Whether you're passionate about data, AI, or streamlining development, there's a challenge here for you.

🎯 Key Details

Submission Period May 6, 2025 - June 17, 2025
Total Prize Amount $50,000
Max Team Size 4 eligible team members
What to Submit Demo video, hosted project URL, code repository, Devpost form

🚀 What You'll Build

In this hackathon, you'll get to explore the power of Google Cloud as you build a project for one (or both!) of the partner competition challenges. Discover the strength of sophisticated data analytics, reliable storage, and comprehensive security solutions, all strategically designed to ignite innovation.

As a multi-partner hackathon, you'll have access to both Google Cloud and either MongoDB or GitLab's platforms for your project, depending on which challenge you choose.

🍃

MongoDB Challenge: Unleash the Power of Data

Harness MongoDB's intelligent data platform designed to power AI-driven solutions. Focus on flexible architecture and rich ecosystem for AI applications using MongoDB Vector Search and Google integrations.

What to Build

Select any public dataset and use AI for analysis alongside MongoDB's powerful search capabilities to create solutions that help users understand and interact with data.

💡 Inspiration Ideas

  • Build applications that provide insightful summaries of complex climate reports
  • Create personalized travel recommendations based on millions of user reviews
  • Design real-time analysis of business sentiment from news articles
  • Design visualizations that reveal hidden connections in social networks
🦊

GitLab Challenge: Accelerating Development

GitLab is the most comprehensive AI-powered DevSecOps platform for software innovation. Use GitLab to build great software faster while improving operational efficiency and security.

What to Build

Create an AI-enabled app using GitLab and Google Cloud. Submissions are encouraged to include contributions to GitLab's Community Edition or functional components for the GitLab CI/CD Catalog.

💡 Inspiration Ideas

  • Develop a deployment pipeline that predicts and fixes bottlenecks
  • Build an AI system that learns from production incidents
  • Create an AI that turns user stories into working prototypes
  • Design a system that automatically parallelizes test suites for 10x faster builds
  • Build an AI mentor that suggests personalized learning paths

🎯 How to Get Started

1. Register

Sign up for the hackathon on Devpost at ai-in-action.devpost.com

2. Get Google Cloud Access

Sign up for a no-cost trial or request $25 in credits for existing accounts

3. Choose Your Challenge

Decide whether you'll tackle MongoDB, GitLab, or both challenges

4. Build & Submit

Start building and submit before June 17, 2025 at 2 PM PT

🌟 Why Multi-Partner Hackathons Rock

The multi-partner hackathon model brings together the best of multiple platforms and creates amazing opportunities. You get to experiment with multiple cutting-edge platforms in a single hackathon, explore various technologies and integration possibilities, and create projects that solve real-world problems.

This approach reaches wider audiences, taps into diverse community networks, and creates lasting cross-industry connections. The result? A richer, more dynamic experience where everyone walks away with more than they came with—whether that's new skills, impressive projects, or promising partnerships.
